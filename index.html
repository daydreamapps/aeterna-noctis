Player Quick Start Guide

What is Crusade?

Crusade is a narrative campaign where your army grows over time. Units gain experience, earn Battle Honours (upgrades), and sometimes suffer Battle Scars (penalties). Your victories matter, your losses have consequences, and your heroes can become legends.

The Story So Far

The Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis has entered the Malakor Sector. Its massive warp-wake has anchored six worlds, dragging them toward the system's sun. Three alliances now fight for control...

The Guardians The Despoilers The Marauders
Alliance Philosophy
The Guardians Those who seek to save the sector—or claim its protection for themselves
The Despoilers Those who would see the sector burn, if only to warm themselves by the flames
The Marauders Those who care nothing for worlds or hulks—only plunder

Getting Started

  1. Choose Your Alliance — Coordinate with the campaign organiser to join one of the three factions.
  2. Build Your Order of Battle — Start with a 1000pt roster. You'll expand as the campaign progresses.
  3. Fight for Planetary Control — Your alliance's control percentage determines boon uses per phase.
  4. Register on Administratum — Join the campaign tracker and add your army.

Progressive Escalation: Points increase by +100 pts every 2 weeks within each phase.

Army Limitations (House Rules)

Phase 1: No Epic Characters, no units over 300pts, max 1 Crucible of Champions character

Phase 2: No Epic Characters over 200pts, no units over 400pts, max 2 Crucible of Champions characters (different base)

Phase 3: Max 2 Crucible of Champions characters (different base)

All Phases: Maximum 2 copies of any unit (same datasheet name), excluding Battleline units

Battle Honours: When a unit gains a Battle Honour, roll randomly to determine which upgrade it receives. The first Battle Honour a unit earns may be chosen by the player instead.

Battle Types

The Breach

1000 pts

Phase 0 skirmishes to earn Intel Points. Low stakes—good for learning.

The Interdiction

1000-1500 pts

Raid enemy planets to reduce their control percentage. Uses Maelstrom rules.

Drop enemy below threshold (60% or 30%) to earn +1 RP.

Raiding means you skip the Hulk battle that round.

The Hulk

1000-2000 pts

Main battles for Strategic Sites and Campaign Victory Points. Nachmund rules.

Skirmish

1000 pts

Battles near planets using Maelstrom rules. Victor gains planetary control or increases defence. Earn Notoriety Points.

Open to non-Crusade forces and external players. XP only if both players are in the Crusade.

Strategic Sites

Control of these locations on the Aeterna Noctis grants powerful bonuses to your alliance.

The Bridge

Command and navigation systems

Muster up to 4 Reinforcement waves instead of 3. Does not allow deploying more waves—only greater tactical flexibility when building your Order of Battle.

The Spires

Sensor arrays and communications

Each time an enemy unit arrives from Strategic Reserves, roll D6: on a 5+, that unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.

The Relay

Warp engine stabilizers

Select +1 Strategic Goal at phase start. At phase end, before rewards, discard one Strategic Goal. Warmaster coordinates.

The Hangar

Launch bays and docking access

Perform up to 2 Surgical Deep Strikes per turn. Once per turn, re-roll a Deep Strike test.

Control: Sites are contested via SAP (Strategic Asset Points) earned from Hulk victories

Threshold: Level 3+ = Control (alliance bonus active) | Level 5+ = Domination (+2 CVP)

Battle Scars

When a unit is destroyed, roll D6 (Out of Action test). On a 1, choose: lose a Battle Honour OR gain a Battle Scar.

Remove scars by spending RP (Repair and Recuperate) or using Ferrum IX's boon.

Units with 3 Battle Scars and no Battle Honours are removed permanently from your Order of Battle.

After Each Battle

  • Gain 1 RP
  • Award XP to participating units
  • Mark one unit for Greatness (+3 XP)
  • Roll for Battle Scars (D6 per destroyed unit, scar on 1)
  • Check for rank-ups and select Battle Honours
  • Spend RP if desired
  • Update Administratum
  • Report results to campaign organiser
