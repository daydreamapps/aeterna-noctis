It began without warning.

For ninety-three solar cycles the *Aeterna Noctis* had drifted at the edge of the Mandeville line — a corpse-cathedral of welded hulls, a tomb large enough to swallow moons. Its halls were our battlefield. Its dust, our spoils. We believed we understood it.

We were wrong.

At zero-four-hundred shipboard, the hulk *accelerated*. Not by void-engine, for it has none that function. Not by Warp-tide, for the augurs sang no such song. The thing simply **moved**, as if remembering a course set ten thousand years ago. And as it moved, it began to **close**.

Bulkheads twenty metres thick slammed shut along corridors that had stood open since the Heresy. Atmospheric seals — long since rusted into uselessness — engaged with the perfect, lubricated precision of newly-forged machinery. Whole transit shafts collapsed inward. Whole *decks* folded.

Forces were sundered. Companies that marched as one were cleaved in two by descending blast-doors. Cohorts that held a junction at dusk woke at dawn to find that junction no longer existed — only a wall of black iron, humming with current that had no source.

**The Hulk is herding us.**

Every sealed door, every collapsed gallery, every redirected vent of toxic vapour drives the survivors **core-ward**. Toward a chamber none of our charts marked. Toward an aperture that opened — *opened*, as if waiting — at the precise moment the outer exits sealed.

The core is exposed. The core is waiting.

And the corridors between are now narrow enough that we may only meet each other.

---

**TO ALL COMBATANTS:**

The campaign of attrition is ended. What remains is a knife-fight in a closing fist.

Your forces are cut off. The units that were not at your side when the bulkheads fell are *gone* — pinned behind sealed iron, or crushed beneath collapsing decks, or simply lost to the dark. Their experience lives on in those who remain. Their banners do not.

You will march toward the core. So will your rivals. The Hulk has decided the matter.

Two cycles. Six battles. Three alliances. One opened heart.

Bring only what you can carry. Bring only those who answered the muster.

The Hulk is closing.

Make the final blows count.
