House Rules Campaign Specific

Army Limitations by Phase

Phase Restrictions
Phase 1 No Epic Characters, no units over 300pts, max 1 Crucible of Champions character
Phase 2 No Epic Characters over 200pts, no units over 400pts, max 2 Crucible of Champions characters (different base)
Phase 3 Max 2 Crucible of Champions characters (different base)
All Phases Maximum 2 copies of any unit (same datasheet name), excluding Battleline units
Battle Honours Roll randomly to determine upgrades. First Battle Honour may be chosen by the player.

Supply Limit Increase

The Increase Supply Limit requisition grants +100 pts per use (standard rules give +200 pts).

Requisition Points (RP)

Starting RP: 5

Gain: 1 RP after each battle

Maximum: 10 RP (cannot exceed this)

All Requisitions

Requisition Cost Effect
Increase Supply Limit 1 RP +100 pts to your Supply Limit House Rule
Renowned Heroes 1-3 RP Give an Enhancement to a CHARACTER. Cost varies by Enhancement type.
Legendary Veterans 3 RP Instantly rank up a unit to Blooded (if Battle-ready) and give them a Battle Honour.
Rearm and Resupply 1 RP Change a unit's wargear options.
Repair and Recuperate 1-5 RP Remove one Battle Scar. Cost = 1 RP + 1 RP per Battle Honour the unit has.
Fresh Recruits 1-4 RP Add models to an existing unit (up to datasheet maximum). Cost scales with unit size.
Renowned Heroes Cost: 1 RP for basic Enhancements, 2 RP for more powerful ones, 3 RP for the most impactful.

Battle Honours Categories

Battle Traits

Special abilities that improve your unit's capabilities. Each faction has unique Battle Traits tables. Roll or select when earning a Battle Honour.

Weapon Modifications

Upgrades to a unit's weapons. Select one weapon and one modification:

Modification Effect
Heirloom +1 to hit rolls
Masterwork +1 Strength
Deadly +1 Damage
Precision +1 AP
Accurate Re-roll one hit roll per phase
Devastating Re-roll one wound roll per phase

Crusade Relics

Powerful artifacts that can only be given to CHARACTER units. Three tiers exist:

Tier Requirement Power Level
Artificer Relic Any CHARACTER Basic relics
Antiquity Relic Heroic rank or higher Powerful relics
Legendary Relic Legendary rank Most powerful relics

Note: You can only have one of each specific relic in your Order of Battle.

Battle Scars

When a unit fails an Out of Action test, it may gain a Battle Scar. Roll D6 or choose:

1. Crippling Damage

-1 to this unit's Move characteristic.

2. Battle-weary

-1 to this unit's Objective Control characteristic (minimum 0).

3. Fatigued

-1 to Advance and Charge rolls for this unit.

4. Disgraced

-1 to this unit's Leadership characteristic.

5. Mark of Shame

This unit cannot be given any Crusade Relics.

6. Deep Scars

This unit cannot be Marked for Greatness.

Units with 3 Battle Scars and no Battle Honours are removed permanently from your Order of Battle.

Out of Action Tests

When to Test

After a battle, roll a D6 for each unit that was destroyed during the game.

Test Result

  • Roll 2-6: The unit recovers without issue.
  • Roll 1: The unit suffers consequences. Choose one:
    • Lose one Battle Honour (if the unit has any), OR
    • Gain one Battle Scar

Planetary Boons

  • Veridian Reach (per use): +1 to Out of Action tests for one battle
  • Ferrum IX (per use): Remove one Battle Scar for 0 RP

Crusade Points

Crusade Points represent a unit's overall power level. Used for balancing and some campaign mechanics.

Calculating Crusade Points

Source Points
Per Battle Honour +1 CP
Per Battle Scar -1 CP
Per Crusade Relic +1 CP

Total Army Crusade Points = Sum of all units' Crusade Points

Skirmish Rules

Skirmish battles take place on or near the planets of the Malakor Sector.

Battle Format

Aspect Details
Points Always 1000 pts
Rules Maelstrom
Location On or near one of the six planets

Victory Rewards

  • Planetary Control: The victor increases their faction's control of the contested planet.
  • Defence Bonus: If the victor's faction already holds the planet, its defence against future attacks is increased instead.
  • Notoriety Points: Earn Notoriety Points as per Maelstrom rules.

Open Play Rules

Flexible Forces: Skirmish battles can be played with any army, including non-Crusade forces.

External Players: Games played with people outside the campaign can be recorded as Skirmish battles.

XP Restriction: Experience Points are only earned if both players are participating in the Crusade.

Nachmund Campaign Rules

These rules govern battles fought on the Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis.

Battle Points

Earned after each Hulk battle based on result and battle size:

Battle Size Victory Draw Defeat
Incursion (1000 pts) 3 BP 1 BP 0 BP
Strike Force (1500-2000 pts) 5 BP 2 BP 0 BP

Strategic Asset Points (SAP)

SAP determine control of Strategic Sites on the Hulk.

  • Earn SAP: 1 SAP per Hulk victory (2 SAP with Void-Spire 7 Boon)
  • Spend SAP: Invest in a Strategic Site to increase your alliance's control

Control Levels

Each Strategic Site has a Control Level (0-5+) for each alliance:

Control Level Status
0 No presence
1-2 Contested
3-4 Controlled
5+ Dominated

The alliance with the highest Control Level at a site gains its bonus.

Strategic Sites

Site Bonus for Controlling Alliance
The Bridge +1 CP at start of each battle
The Spires Re-roll one hit roll per battle
The Relay +1 to Reserves rolls
The Hangar One unit gains Scout 6" per battle

Campaign Victory Points (CVP)

CVP determine the overall campaign winner.

  • End of Phase: Alliance with most Battle Points earns bonus CVP
  • Strategic Site Control: Controlling sites earns CVP
  • Home World Raids: Successfully disabling enemy Boons earns CVP

Phase Rewards

At the end of each campaign phase:

  • Alliance with most Battle Points: +3 CVP
  • Per Strategic Site controlled (Level 3+): +1 CVP
  • Per Strategic Site dominated (Level 5+): +2 CVP
