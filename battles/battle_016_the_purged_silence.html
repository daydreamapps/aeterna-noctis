Hulk Battle 016

The Purged Silence

Purge After Inload 4 — Cogitator Denial

++ Priority: Crimson ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 830.M42 ++

ALERT: Cogitator info-shrines detected in Sector Phi-7. Machine-spirits within contain intelligence archives of critical strategic value—navigation data, void-routes through the Rift, and records of xenos movements predating the Great Rift's sundering.

The Crusade of Binding Chains has established a defensive perimeter. Marshal Vlackl has declared these shrines sacrosanct—their data must be preserved for the Ecclesiarchy's archives, purged only when properly consecrated. The Black Templars will not suffer the Enemy to corrupt what rightfully belongs to the God-Emperor.

The Sons of Decay approach from the lower decks. Their Biologus Putrifiers have detected something within the cogitators—perhaps echoes of forbidden knowledge, or merely the chance to deny the Imperium another fragment of its crumbling domain. Where the Death Guard tread, nothing remains untainted.

Both forces seek the same objective: inload the data, then purge the shrines. What knowledge will survive? What will be lost to entropy and flame?

Mission Overview

Cogitator info-shrines at this site contain vital intelligence that must be accessed and eradicated to deny it to the foe. Forces must drop in swiftly, inload all essential intelligence, and then purge the shrines by any means necessary.

Mission Purge After Inload 4
Battle Size Incursion (1000pts)
Objectives 4 (Info-Shrines)
Battle Rounds 5

Mission Rules

Infopurge (Action)

Access the cogitator shrine, extract what data you can, then purge it to deny the enemy.

Phase Requirement
Starts Your Shooting phase, from the second battle round onwards
Units One unit from your Crusade army within range of an objective marker that is not within your own Deployment Zone
Completes End of your opponent's next turn, or the end of the battle (whichever comes first), if you control that objective marker
If Completed: That objective marker is purged and removed from the battlefield.

Mission Objectives

Info-Shrine Inload

As long as the info-shrines can be held, more intelligence can be gathered.

Progressive Objective — From the second battle round onwards, at the end of each player's Command phase:

Condition VP
Score 1VP for each objective marker you control (to a maximum of 2VP) 1-2 VP
In the fifth battle round, the player who has the second turn scores VP as described above, but does so at the end of the turn instead of at the end of their Command phase.

Infopurge

Keep the foe ignorant, even at the expense of your enlightenment.

Progressive Objective — Each time a player whose turn it is purges an objective marker:

Location VP
Objective marker is within your own territory 2 VP
Objective marker is within your opponent's territory 3 VP

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one unit from their Crusade army that purged one or more objective markers during the battle to gain a Battle Trait.

  • This must be a unit that can gain a Battle Trait.
Bonus: If the victor purged the objective marker within their opponent's deployment zone, they gain 1 SAP.

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

  • Final Score: Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
  • Objectives Purged: Which player purged which objectives, and where
  • Kills: Which of your units destroyed which enemy units
  • Casualties: Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
  • Notable Moments: 2-3 memorable events for the battle report
