AFTER-ACTION REPORT: Sector Phi-7 engagement concluded. Outcome: INCONCLUSIVE. Cogitator shrines status: COMPROMISED. Intelligence recovery: PARTIAL.

The battle devolved into mutual annihilation. The Death Guard's Foetid Bloat-drone reaped a terrible harvest with three kills before its diseased hull finally ruptured, while the Lord of Virulence's calculated strikes earned him recognition among Grandfather Nurgle's favoured. The Black Templars' Inceptor Squad descended like avenging angels, their plasma fire cutting through the putrid horde.

Casualties were catastrophic on both sides—eleven Death Guard units fell to Black Templar fury, eight Crusaders joined the Emperor's side. Both Marshals emerged scarred: the Chaos Rhino bears new corruption, while the Black Templar Marshal carries wounds that will not easily heal.

The shrines burn. The data is lost to both sides. In the silence that follows, only the dead remain to guard secrets now reduced to ash.

"In mutual destruction, both sides claim defeat."
