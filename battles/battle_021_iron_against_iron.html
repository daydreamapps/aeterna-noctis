They have come again. The Killing Stone — those fell warriors who ground the Adeptus Mechanicus to a five-four rout in the rust-clogged corridors of Battle 017 — have returned to press their advantage deeper into the hulk's iron guts. Where once they merely contested the approaches, now they seek to run the full gauntlet of the Omnissiah's servants, driving spear-tip forces through overlapping fields of galvanic fire toward the very heart of Mechanicus-held territory.

Arch-Magos Secondary Valior-9 has prepared his defences in exquisite depth. Three lines of skitarii anchor each objective node in interlocking kill-corridors. His combat doctrina calculates a 73.4% probability that no enemy unit penetrates to the Gamma Zone intact. The data is precise. The data is confident.

But the Word Bearers who counsel The Killing Stone do not care for data. They care for momentum — for the grinding weight of bodies that do not stop, of warriors who feel no pain and acknowledge no tactical retreat. The gauntlet was designed to be run. The question is only what survives the crossing.

"The Omnissiah built walls of iron. We are made of the same."

Mission Overview

Mission: The Gauntlet 5 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Whether driving home an attack against a loyalist strongpoint or seeking to eliminate an exposed heretic base, many offensives see attackers run a gauntlet of potent enemy defences.

Mission Rule — Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Mission Rule — Punch Through Their Defences

At the end of the Attacker's turn, after scoring VP, any objective markers controlled by the Attacker are removed from the battlefield.

Mission Rule — Up the Middle

Units from the Attacker's army arriving from Tactical Reserves cannot be set up within the Defender's deployment zone.

Victor Bonus

The victors' Crusade force gains 1RP and 1SAP.

Deployment Zones

Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Zones advance from the Attacker's deployment edge toward the Defender's lines.

Mission Objectives

Layered Defence — Defender's Progressive Objective

Defence in depth sees the attacking forces strung out.

At the end of the Defender's turn, they score VP as follows:

  • If they control 3 or more objective markers, they score 1VP.
  • If there are no enemy units within the Gamma Zone, the Defender scores 1VP.
  • If one or more enemy units were destroyed that turn, the Defender scores 1VP.

Run the Gauntlet — Attacker's Progressive Objective

The attackers drive their spear into the heart of the foe.

At the end of the Attacker's turn, they score VP as follows:

  • For each objective marker they control, they score 1VP.
  • They score additional VP based on how deep their Primary Wave units have advanced — if one or more units are wholly within:
    • The Alpha Zone: 1VP
    • The Beta Zone: 2VP
    • The Gamma Zone: 3VP
Only the highest zone bonus counts — scoring Beta Zone does not also grant the Alpha Zone bonus.
