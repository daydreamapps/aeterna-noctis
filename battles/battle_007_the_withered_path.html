The Withered Path

++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 822.M42 ++

AFTER-ACTION REPORT — ENGAGEMENT: SUPPLY RAID. The Bahzhakhain Süirel drove hard into the corridors of the Bleeding Core, their Corsair blades and wraith-constructs seeking the supply caches of Sector Omicron. They found the Sons of Decay waiting. They did not leave.

Sephraxis the Blight-Crowned prosecuted the engagement with merciless precision, felling four Aeldari across the engagement and accruing nine marks of experience in a single action. The Tallyman Morbidus Rottcount, ever faithful in accounting, was elevated beyond his station — his tally sheets now listing ten destroyed xenos units to the glory of the Plague God.

The Aeldari were expunged wholesale. All ten Bahzhakhain Süirel units were destroyed — their Warp Spiders, though marked for greatness in death, found only annihilation. The Rotbound Host and Noxious Chariot were broken in the exchange, and one Plague Marines division ground to dust. A tolerable tithe. The supply caches belong to the rot now.

"Every road leads to Nurgle. The Aeldari simply arrived sooner."
— Morbidus Rottcount, Final Tally of the Withered Path
