Hulk Battle 012

Echoes of the Silenced

Purge After Inload 4 — Nachmund Gauntlet

++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 826.M42 ++

INTERCEPTED TRANSMISSION: Cogitator shrines detected in Sector Tau-7. Data-cores contain pre-Heresy navigational charts and xenos encryption protocols of significant strategic value. Multiple factions have been alerted to this discovery.

The Trans-Eternians Alliance approaches from the outer corridors. The Kin move with purpose—their Votann ancestors demand the data be secured or destroyed rather than fall into rival hands. Their grudges run deep, and the Necrons have earned their ire.

The Tekhsarun Awakening stirs in response. These ancient machines view the cogitator shrines as their rightful property—echoes of technology they mastered when humanity was yet to crawl from Terra's cradle. They will not yield their claim easily.

Two xenos forces. One prize. The info-shrines will either be inloaded and purged, or they will become the graves of those who sought them.

Mission Overview

Cogitator info-shrines at this site contain vital intelligence that must be accessed and eradicated to deny it to the foe. Forces must drop in swiftly, inload all essential intelligence, and then purge the shrines by any means necessary.

Board Size 60" x 44"
Objectives 5 Info-Shrines
Deployment Hammer and Anvil
Centre Exclusion 6" Radius

Deployment Zones

Mission Rules

Infopurge (Action)

Phase Your Shooting phase, from the second battle round onwards
Units One unit from your Crusade army that is within range of an objective marker that is not within your own Deployment Zone
Completes End of your opponent's next turn or the end of the battle (whichever comes first), if you control that objective marker
If Completed That objective marker is purged and removed from the battlefield
Purged objectives are removed entirely! Plan your scoring carefully—once an info-shrine is purged, it cannot be controlled by either player.

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Info-Shrine Inload

As long as the info-shrines can be held, more intelligence can be gathered.

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
For each objective marker you control (max 2VP) 1 VP each
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

Progressive Objective: Infopurge

Keep the foe ignorant, even at the expense of your enlightenment.

Each time you purge an objective marker:

Condition VP
Purge an objective in your own territory 2 VP
Purge an objective in your opponent's territory 3 VP

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one unit from their Crusade army that purged one or more objective markers during the battle to gain a Battle Trait. This must be a unit that can gain a Battle Trait.

Bonus SAP: If the victor purged the objective marker within their opponent's deployment zone, they gain +1 SAP.

Army Setup: Tactical Reserves

Nachmund Gauntlet uses Tactical Reserves — your army is split into waves that arrive throughout the battle.

Incursion (1000pts) Wave Configuration

Wave Points Limit Arrives
Primary Wave Up to 500 pts Deployment (start of battle)
Reinforcement Wave 1 Up to 250 pts Start of Battle Round 2
Reinforcement Wave 2 Up to 250 pts Start of Battle Round 3
Your total army can exceed 1000pts — the wave limits are separate. Plan your Order of Battle accordingly.

Reinforcement Arrival Options

When a Reinforcement Wave arrives, each unit in that wave can arrive via one of the following methods:

  • Battlefield Edge Arrival: Set up wholly within 6" of your battlefield edge and more than 9" from enemy models
  • Deep Strike (if unit has ability): Use the unit's Deep Strike ability as normal, arriving more than 9" from enemy models
  • Surgical Deep Strike (1CP Stratagem): One unit per wave can Deep Strike 3" closer (6" from enemies instead of 9"). Must have Deep Strike ability.

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

Final Score Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
Objectives Purged Which units purged which objectives, and in whose territory
Kills Which of your units destroyed which enemy units (for "Dealers of Death" XP: +1 XP per 3 units destroyed)
Casualties Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
XP Earned Per unit: +1 XP (participation) + Agenda XP + Dealers of Death + Marked for Greatness (if applicable)
Notable Moments 2-3 memorable events for the battle report (heroic last stands, dramatic kills, lucky/unlucky dice, etc.)
