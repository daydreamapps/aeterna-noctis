Hulk Battle 006

Wrath Unchained

Nachmund Gauntlet — Strategic Strike

Battle Report

Despoilers Victory 14 — 12
++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 821.M42 ++

The wrath was indeed unchained—but it was not enough. In a battle that will be sung of for generations, the Crusade of Binding Chains hurled themselves against the Killing Stone's fortified position with righteous fury, yet fell just short of victory. The final tally: 14-12 to the heretics.

The Emperor's Champion, Brother Aldric, fought with transcendent skill. A twelve-inch charge through the carnage saw him cut down both the Accursed Cultists and the Dark Commune in a single devastating assault. Later, when the Chaos Predator seemed unstoppable, Aldric's blade found its mark—a desperate reroll yielding devastating wounds that silenced its guns forever. The Pyre of Heretics matched his zeal, their own impossible charge destroying the Chaos Bikers before they could react.

Yet the Despoilers held. The Chainbreakers carved through Raptors and Venomcrawler alike, but the Legionaries and their Chaos Lord anchored the line. Silent Judgement fell, their survivors bearing the mark of fatigue. In the end, the Black Templars' sacred Oaths of Crusade remained unfulfilled. The Despoilers control the drop zone. Two victories in succession—the corruption spreads.
