ENGAGEMENT IMMINENT: Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis — Sub-sector Malakor. Mechanicus assault column advancing on entrenched Despoiler position. Designation: THE BOTTOMLESS BUCKET. Affiliation: Guardians. Threat classification: Extreme.

Fresh from their hard-won advance at the Iron Shore, the Adeptus Mechanicus press their momentum deeper into the hulk's corroded innards. The Magi have calculated a high-probability vector — a gauntlet of sealed bulkheads and suppurating kill-zones that leads, according to their cogitators, to a node of strategic significance. The Omnissiah's faithful will run that gauntlet. They do not know how to do otherwise.

The Sons of Decay have had time to prepare. Where another army might fortify with sandbags and wire, Mortarion's sons fortify with rot. The corridors ahead reek of Nurgle's blessings — layered defences of bloated flesh and corroded iron that have repelled no fewer than three assaults in the last solar week. The machines of the Bottomless Bucket will be tested against a wall that does not break. It only festers.

Mission Overview

Mission: The Gauntlet 5 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Whether driving home an attack against a loyalist strongpoint or seeking to eliminate an exposed heretic base, many offensives see attackers run a gauntlet of potent enemy defences.

Mission Rules

Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Punch Through Their Defences

At the end of the Attacker's turn, after scoring VP, any objective markers controlled by the Attacker are removed from the battlefield.

Up the Middle

Units from the Attacker's army arriving from Tactical Reserves cannot be set up within the Defender's deployment zone.

Deployment

Five objective markers arranged across three zones: Alpha Zone (Attacker's side), Beta Zone (centre), and Gamma Zone (Defender's side).

Mission Objectives

Layered Defence — Progressive (Defender)

Defence in depth sees the attacking forces strung out.

  • At the end of the Defender's turn, if they control 3 or more objective markers, they score 1VP.
  • At the end of the Defender's turn, if there are no enemy units within the Gamma Zone, the Defender scores 1VP.
  • At the end of the Defender's turn, if one or more enemy units were destroyed that turn, the Defender scores 1VP.

Run the Gauntlet — Progressive (Attacker)

The attackers drive their spear into the heart of the foe.

  • At the end of the Attacker's turn, for each objective marker they control, they score 1VP.
  • At the end of the Attacker's turn, they score VP if one or more units from their Primary Wave are wholly within:
    • the Alpha Zone — 1VP
    • the Beta Zone — 2VP
    • the Gamma Zone — 3VP

Victor Bonus

The victor's Crusade force gains 1RP and 1SAP.

Tactical Reserves

Up the Middle: Attacker's Tactical Reserves cannot arrive in the Defender's deployment zone. Plan your Reserve drops accordingly.

Both commanders should declare their Tactical Reserve units before the battle begins.
