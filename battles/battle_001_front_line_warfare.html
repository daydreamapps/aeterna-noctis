Hulk Battle 001

Front-line Warfare

Nachmund Gauntlet Mission

++ Priority: Vermillion ++ ++ Source: Astropathic Relay Sigma-7 ++ ++ Clearance: Warmaster Eyes Only ++

The Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis groans with ancient fury as two forces converge upon Sector Theta-9. Auspex readings confirm weapons discharge imminent.

The Unforgiven have come. Veterans of The Judgement of Aldurukh deploy from the battle barge Capitis Damnare, their dark armour drinking the emergency lumens. They seek to secure vital bulkheads for the Guardians' advance toward the Hulk's command systems.

Yet the void-kin bar their path. The Trans-Eternians Alliance has calculated significant value in this derelict's corridors. Their Votann demands resources, and grudges are already being recorded against any who would claim what the Kin have marked for acquisition.

This is the first major engagement aboard the Aeterna Noctis. Whoever seizes these front-line positions will control the flow of reinforcements for cycles to come.

Mission Overview

Board Size 60" x 44"
Objectives 6 Total
Deployment Diagonal
Centre Exclusion 9" Radius

Deployment Zones

Objective Placement

Setup: Starting with the Defender, players alternate placing 6 objective markers.

Placement Rules

  • 1 objective must be wholly within each deployment zone (2 total)
  • 4 objectives must be wholly within No Man's Land
  • Objectives must be 6"+ away from other objectives
  • Objectives must be 6"+ away from battlefield edges
If an objective cannot be placed following these rules, it is not set up.

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Commanding Position

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
Per objective controlled in No Man's Land 1 VP
Control objective in opponent's deployment zone 2 VP
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

End Game Objective: Line of Advance

At the end of the battle:

Condition VP
Control BOTH your deployment zone objective AND your opponent's deployment zone objective 3 VP

Army Setup: Tactical Reserves

Nachmund Gauntlet uses Tactical Reserves — your army is split into waves that arrive throughout the battle.

Incursion (1000pts) Wave Configuration

Wave Points Limit Arrives
Primary Wave Up to 400 pts Deployment (start of battle)
Reinforcement Wave 1 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 2
Reinforcement Wave 2 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 3
Your total army can exceed 1000pts — the wave limits are separate. Plan your Order of Battle accordingly.

Building Your Waves

  • Assign each unit to a wave before the battle
  • Reinforcement waves arrive from your battlefield edge (or via Deep Strike if the unit has that ability)
  • You choose which reinforcement wave arrives at the start of each round
Full Tactical Reserves rules on Wahapedia

Agenda Selection

Before the battle, select 2 Agendas for your army. Units can earn XP by completing Agenda objectives.

Option 1: Nachmund Agendas

Role-specific agendas. Choose based on whether you are Attacker or Defender.

Defender Agendas (3)

Activate Defence Perimeter

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in your deployment zone performs "Activate Shield Node" (Shooting phase). Completes end of opponent's next turn.

+1 XP per node

+1 SAP if 4+ nodes

Repel the Foe

Destroy enemy Reinforcement wave units.

+1 XP per kill (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if entire wave wiped

Defiant to the End

Select up to 3 units at battle start.

+2 XP if survives, +1 XP if above half

+1 SAP if any at Starting Strength

Attacker Agendas (3)

Symbolic Objectives

Opponent selects 2 objectives. Control them, select up to 3 units within range at end.

+2 XP per selected unit

+1 SAP if control all selected

Prime Macro-Ordnance

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in enemy deployment zone performs "Prime Planetquake Bomb" (Shooting phase).

+2 XP per completion (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if completed 2+ times

Raze and Ruin

Destroy enemies near objectives.

+1 XP per kill (+2 XP if enemy zone). Max 3 XP/unit

+1 SAP if control all enemy objectives

Option 2: Faction-Specific Agendas

Dark Angels — The Judgement of Aldurukh (8)

Dark Angels Specific

Dark Rumour

Kill the enemy Warlord with a DEATHWING, RAVENWING, or INNER CIRCLE unit.

+3 XP to the unit that kills the Warlord

Encircle the Foe

At end of battle, have 3-4 RAVENWING units each wholly within 9" of a different battlefield corner.

+2 XP per qualifying unit

The Deathwing Cometh

DEATHWING unit destroys enemy units on the turn it teleports in.

+2 XP (+3 XP if destroyed a CHARACTER)

Mental Interrogation

Action: LIBRARIAN performs psychic interrogation against enemy CHARACTER within 12".

+1 XP per successful interrogation

Adeptus Astartes Generic

Angels of Death

At end of battle, if no enemy units remain on battlefield, each ADEPTUS ASTARTES unit on the battlefield scores.

+2 XP per unit

Know No Fear

At end of battle, each ADEPTUS ASTARTES unit that did not fail a Battle-shock test scores.

+1 XP (+2 XP if also Below Half-strength)

Armoured Assault

At end of battle, VEHICLE units that survived OR were destroyed but killed 2+ enemies score.

+1 XP per qualifying vehicle

Quest of Atonement

Select unit with Battle-weary, Disgraced, or Mark of Shame. If it destroys a CHARACTER/MONSTER/VEHICLE in melee, remove one scar.

+5 XP and lose one Battle Scar

Votann

XP for completing the acquisition

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

Final Score Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
Kills Which of your units destroyed which enemy units (for "Dealers of Death" XP: +1 XP per 3 units destroyed)
Casualties Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
XP Earned Per unit: +1 XP (participation) + Agenda XP + Dealers of Death + Marked for Greatness (if applicable)
Notable Moments 2-3 memorable events for the battle report (heroic last stands, dramatic kills, lucky/unlucky dice, etc.)
