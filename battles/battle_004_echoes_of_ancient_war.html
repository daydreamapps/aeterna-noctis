Battle Briefing

++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Marauders Alliance Intercept ++ ++ Timestamp: 819.M42 ++

The Necrons of the Rattle me Bones have entered the Malakor Sector. Their tomb ships drift at the edge of the system, ancient sensors probing the warp-wake of the Aeterna Noctis. But the Overlords do not move on the Space Hulk. Not yet.

Instead, they turn their gaze to older matters. The Aeldari of Bahzhakhain Süirel hold territories that the Necrons remember—worlds that bore different names sixty million years ago, before the War in Heaven reduced them to ash. The grudge-crypts have been unsealed. The ancient war protocols reactivated.

While the Guardians and Despoilers tear each other apart aboard the Hulk, the Marauders settle their own scores. The Necrons will strike swiftly, seizing the drop zone before the Aeldari can mount a proper defence. There will be no mercy. There never was.

Mission Rules

Drop Site Secure

Each time a unit performs a Surgical Deep Strike, if that unit is set up wholly within its own Deployment Zone, add +2 to that unit's Deep Strike test.

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one unit from their Crusade army that is wholly within their opponent's deployment zone at the end of the battle; that unit gains +5 XP.

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Seize Ground

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
Per objective marker controlled (max 3) 1 VP each
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

Progressive Objective: Breakthrough Push

At the end of your turn:

Condition VP
One or more units (not Battle-shocked) wholly within opponent's deployment zone 1 VP
Bonus: If one or more of those units are BATTLELINE +1 VP

Army Setup: Tactical Reserves

Nachmund Gauntlet uses Tactical Reserves — your army is split into waves that arrive throughout the battle.

Incursion (1000pts) Wave Configuration

Wave Points Limit Arrives
Primary Wave Up to 400 pts Deployment (start of battle)
Reinforcement Wave 1 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 2
Reinforcement Wave 2 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 3
Your total army can exceed 1000pts — the wave limits are separate. Plan your Order of Battle accordingly.

Building Your Waves

  • Assign each unit to a wave before the battle
  • Reinforcement waves arrive from your battlefield edge (or via Deep Strike if the unit has that ability)
  • You choose which reinforcement wave arrives at the start of each round

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

Final Score Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
Kills Which of your units destroyed which enemy units (for "Dealers of Death" XP: +1 XP per 3 units destroyed)
Casualties Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
XP Earned Per unit: +1 XP (participation) + Agenda XP + Dealers of Death + Marked for Greatness (if applicable)
Notable Moments 2-3 memorable events for the battle report (heroic last stands, dramatic kills, lucky/unlucky dice, etc.)
