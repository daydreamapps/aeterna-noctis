Chokepoint at Lambda-3

Nachmund Gauntlet Mission

Battle Report

DESPOILERS VICTORY 10 — 7
++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Sector Lambda-3 Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 816.M42 ++

The chokepoint at Lambda-3 has fallen to the Ruinous Powers. High Marshal Grimvow is down—struck by the Venomcrawler's bile-cannons as his warriors attempted to secure the central junction. The Pilgrim's Burden limped from the field on a single functioning tread, its machine spirit scarred by the ordeal.

Yet the Templars did not break. The Pyre of Heretics became the blade of the Emperor's wrath—Initiate-Sergeant Volker Brandtfist led his dwindling squad through four enemy units before the Predator's guns finally silenced them. The Neophytes learned fury before caution. They learned it well.

And in the final hours, Captain Hektor the Unbroken earned his epithet anew. Alone amid the wreckage, the Gravis-armoured warrior endured two full rounds of concentrated fire from the remaining Chaos forces—Venomcrawler, Bikers, Legionaries—and refused to fall. Three times declared dead. Three times his hearts beat on.

The enemy's Venomcrawler and Predator proved decisive, their firepower strangling every Imperial advance. The Chaos warbands now control the staging posts. The Guardians must find another route.

The Chapel of Eternal Vigil has chosen. Brother-Initiate Caden's sacrifice at Aethelgard has been answered—a new Emperor's Champion rises, blessed with the Epitome of Piety and marked as an Inspirational Fighter.

"Even in death, duty endures."
— Thought for the Day

Mission Rules

Staging Post

CP Restriction: Players only gain 1 CP during their turn if they control the objective marker within their own deployment zone.

If you do not control your deployment zone objective, you do not gain CP that turn. Secure your staging post or be starved of resources!

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Ruthless Aggression

At the end of your turn:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers that were controlled by your opponent at the start of the turn 1 VP
Bonus: If one or more of those objectives were in opponent's deployment zone +1 VP
Key: You score for taking objectives from your opponent—not just holding them. Aggression is rewarded!

Progressive Objective: Domination

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers 1 VP
Control more objective markers than your opponent 2 VP
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

Selected Agendas

Black Templars — The Crusade of Binding Chains (Attacker)
Agenda 1

Reconsecration

After deployment, opponent selects one objective (not in their zone) to be your Holy Relic. At end of your turn, if you control it, select one unit within range.

+3 XP to selected unit. If you control it at battle end: +D3 Relic Fragments, +1 Honour. If opponent controls it: lose 1 RP.

Agenda 2

First-Hand Experience

Each time a CRUSADER SQUAD destroys an enemy unit, that squad gains 1 XP.

+1 XP per kill (max 3 XP/unit). +1 bonus XP if Neophytes survive. +2 Honour if any unit gained 2+ XP.

Bonus Agenda

Oaths of Crusade

Create a custom oath by selecting: 1 Deed + 1 Foe + optional Passion + optional Condition.

Deeds: Bring glory (be victor), Shatter holdings (control enemy objective), Reclaim relics (control more objectives).

Foes: Lay low tyrants (kill CHARACTER/WARLORD), Strike down foes (kill MONSTER/VEHICLE/TITANIC), Slaughter faithless (kill 3+/6+ units).

Complete all parts: gain all listed rewards (Relic Fragments, Honour, XP). Random selection: +1 Honour bonus.

Oathsworn Campaign: The Crusade of Binding Chains is on an active crusade. The Oaths of Crusade bonus agenda must be taken each battle. Use the Devotional Codicil Requisition (1RP) once per campaign to add up to 2 Passions and 2 Conditions to your oath.
Chaos Space Marines — Heretic Astartes (Defender)
Agenda 1

Blasphemous Ritual

Select up to 3 HERETIC ASTARTES INFANTRY units at battle start. Each time one makes a Dark Pact and passes the Leadership test, that unit gains 1 XP.

+1 XP per passed Dark Pact (max 3 XP/unit). If all selected units gain 3 XP: +1 Dark God Glory.

Agenda 2

Search Drop Site

Action: One unit within range of an unsearched objective marker. Starts: Shooting phase. Completes: End of turn if you control that marker.

+3 XP per searched objective. If 3+ objectives searched: +1 SAP.
