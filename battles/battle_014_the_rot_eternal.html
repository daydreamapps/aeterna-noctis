The Rot Eternal

Stranglehold 2 — Nachmund Gauntlet

++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 828.M42 ++

AFTER-ACTION REPORT: Sector Lambda-9 has fallen to the Sons of Decay. The Tekhsarun Awakening's forces have been routed. Grandfather's blessings now seep into every corridor.

The Necrons awakened sluggish this day—their reanimation protocols failing to compensate for whatever ancient slumber still gripped their processes. The Immortals, pride of the awakening, were annihilated in a single devastating phase before they could bring their gauss weaponry to bear. The Lord of Virulence proved the architect of this massacre, claiming six kills as his plague weapons found their marks with terrible precision.

The Deathshroud Terminators carved through three enemy units, their scythes reaping a harvest worthy of Mortarion himself. Despite their living metal bodies being immune to disease, the Necrons found no defense against bolter, blade, and blight.

Sector Lambda-9 is now a shrine to entropy. The rot eternal has claimed another victory.

"Even the deathless can be broken. Even metal rusts in time."

Notable Moment

The Sleepy Awakening

The Necrons must have still been drowsy from their aeons of slumber...

In what can only be described as a catastrophic systems failure, the Immortals—ancient warriors who have outlasted civilizations—were annihilated in a single devastating phase. Their gauss weapons never fired. Their reanimation protocols never activated. The Lord of Virulence's plague weapons found every weakness in their living metal forms.

Despite this ignominious end, the Immortals were marked for greatness—perhaps the Overlord hopes this humiliation will drive them to greater heights when they next awaken.
