ENGAGEMENT RESOLVED: Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis — Sub-sector Malakor. Hidden stronghold held. Victor: THE SONS OF DECAY. Final tally: twelve to three.

The Aeldari came as saboteurs. Jain Zar herself led the assault through the rust-choked corridors, with Eldrad Ulthran threading the skeins of fate behind her. They came to cripple the Despoilers' operational base. They came to destruction. The Plague Lord claimed three kills before the first wave was spent. The Deathshroud moved through the dark like plague made manifest — Rangers silenced, the ancient Ghost Warrior unmade, the Falcon reduced to cooling slag. The Chaos Spawn ran down Eldrad himself: the great seer's barrier dissolved in ichor, and he was dragged under.

Wave two of the Aeldari arrived and was annihilated. The Deathshroud claimed the Fire Dragons before they could perform a single sabotage action — the squad earned only disgrace, their honour stripped, unable to be marked for their failure. Jain Zar fell. The Howling Banshees were destroyed to the last, marked in death for the single kill they achieved before the rot consumed them.

The Tallyman endured and was honoured. The Plague Lord [Crucible] fell but was Marked for Greatness in destruction. The stronghold stands. Malakor belongs to the plague.

"They came to sabotage our works. We gave them a more permanent assignment."
— Recovered parchment, Sons of Decay command sanctum

Mission Rules

Mission: Saboteurs 2 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Mission Rule — Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Mission Rule — Supply Sabotage

The Attacker can perform the Supply Sabotage Action: one or more units, each within range of a different objective marker, beginning in their Shooting phase. The action completes at the end of the opponent's next turn (or end of battle) if that objective is still controlled. Sabotaged objectives are removed from the battlefield, scoring 3VP each. If 2+ are sabotaged in the same turn, score an additional 1VP.

Victor Bonus

The victor's Crusade force gains 1 Requisition Point and 1 Strategic Asset Point.

The Sons of Decay claimed the Victor Bonus: +1 RP, +1 SAP.
