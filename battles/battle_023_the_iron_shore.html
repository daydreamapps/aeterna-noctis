ENGAGEMENT RESOLVED: Space Hulk Aeterna Noctis — Sub-sector Malakor. Beachhead established. Victor: THE BOTTOMLESS BUCKET. Final tally: eleven to eight.

The Adeptus Mechanicus came with cold calculation, and cold calculation prevailed. The Skorpius Dunerider swept the forward approach, searching drop sites before the Aeldari could contest them — its contribution to the Omnissiah's databanks measured in territory secured and a character's skull crushed beneath its treads. The Terrax-Pattern Termite bored into the heart of the Aeldari lines, its passengers shredding the last of the Dark Reapers before being torn apart by the Wraithguard's implacable advance. The Ghost Warrior warlord fell to Kastelan fists. Three objectives searched. The beachhead calcified.

The Aeldari fought with the fury of the dying. Jain Zar and the Howling Banshees arrived late and hit like a blade in the dark — three kills, two Admech units unmade in a single turn, Marked for Greatness in victory's shadow. It was not enough. The margin was three. The corridors belong to the Omnissiah now.

The Servitor Battleclade endured without glory and was honoured for it. The Skorpius Dunerider was singled out above all — Marked for Greatness. The Bottomless Bucket claims its beachhead.

"The calculation was never in doubt. Only the rounding error."
— Recovered parchment, Bottomless Bucket command node

Mission Rules

Mission: Beachhead Offensive 4 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Beachhead

Attacker deploys all Primary Wave units first; then the Defender deploys all Primary Wave units.

Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Secure Beachhead — Progressive

  • From the second battle round onwards, at the end of the Command phase, the active player scores 1VP per objective marker controlled.
  • If it is the Attacker's turn and they control their own deployment zone objective, each additional objective they control scores +1VP (doubled).
  • In the fifth battle round, the second player scores at the end of their turn instead of Command phase.

Victor Bonus

The victor selects one additional unit to be Marked for Greatness.

The Bottomless Bucket claimed the Victor Bonus: +1 additional Marked for Greatness.
