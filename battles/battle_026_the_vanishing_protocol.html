AFTER-ACTION REPORT: Retrieval operation concluded. Outcome: INCONCLUSIVE. Courier status: UNRECOVERED. Intelligence remains at large within Aeterna Noctis.

The Tekhsarun Awakening's Flayed Ones located the courier in Round 2, claiming the search grid and holding the objective through sustained reanimation and attrition. The Bottomless Bucket tore through the Necron command structure with methodical precision — Overlord, Plasmancer, Reanimator, Technomancer, Ammentar; the tomb lords fell one by one to Skitarii fire and the relentless advance of the Termite's bore-drill — yet the objective marker never changed hands long enough to shift the score. A Cybernetica Datasmith earned its battle honours with three confirmed kills and is now Marked for Greatness by the Fabricator-Marshal's cogitators.

The engagement's final act proved fitting. The last Kastelan Robot — crippled and near-critical — was gutted by the Skorpekh Lord that had haunted it all battle. Its reactor detonation consumed them both. The corridor fell silent. The courier was gone. The data remains unaccounted for.

"The search is never over. It only changes hands."
— Final log entry, Retrieval Cohort Vanguard, 840.M42
