PRE-ENGAGEMENT BRIEFING: Sector Omega-Null, deep within the Aeterna Noctis's corrupted mid-decks. Conflict classification: FRATRICIDAL. Both forces bear the seal of the Guardians alliance. Cause: DISPUTED.

The Crusade of Binding Chains has been driven back. The Black Templars — their advance shattered by forces unknown — have retreated to a fortified enclave deep in the mid-decks, a redoubt of adamantine bulkheads and sanctified killing grounds. Marshal-Dreadnought Valdric has sealed the gates and refused extraction. He will hold this stronghold or he will die within it. There is no third scripture.

The Judgement of Aldurukh converges from two directions simultaneously. The Deathwing's movements were never random — the pincer was prepared before the first shot was fired. They advance through Attack Corridors Sigma and Tau, cutting off all avenues of relief, ensuring that whatever emerges from those fortified halls is theirs to judge.

What purpose drives the Lion's sons to crush their own allies into rubble? The sensorium offers no answer. Only the clash of ceramite and the echo of oaths broken in the darkness.

"What does the lion hunt, when all prey has fled? Its own shadow."
— Inscription recovered from Sector Omega-Null bulkhead, origin unknown

Mission Overview

Mission: Vital Stronghold 6 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

An isolated force has been driven back to a pivotal defensive enclave. Will they hold out long enough to be reinforced, or will converging enemy assault groups crush them and claim their bolthole?

Mission Rule — Pincer Attack

At the start of the Reinforcements step of the Defender's Movement phase in the second battle round, the Defender must select two of their Reinforcement Waves to arrive from Tactical Reserves, but will not be able to select any other Reinforcement Waves to arrive that battle.

  • Each unit from one of those Reinforcement Waves must be set up wholly within Attack Zone 1
  • Each unit from the other Reinforcement Wave must be set up wholly within Attack Zone 2
  • One unit from one of the selected Reinforcement Waves may perform a Surgical Deep Strike, however that unit must be set up wholly within Attack Zone 1 or Attack Zone 2

Mission Rule — Stronghold

The Defender must set up five additional objective markers on the battlefield:

  • Two or more objective markers must be set up in No Man's Land
  • Each objective marker cannot be set up within 6" of one or more other objective markers and/or one or more battlefield edges
  • If it is not possible to set up an objective marker, it is not set up on the battlefield

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one unit from their Crusade army that is wholly within 6" of the centre of the battlefield; that unit gains 5XP.

Mission Objectives

The Fortress Claimed — Progressive Objective

Victory will almost certainly go to whichever commander holds onto the strongpoint.

From the second battle round onwards, at the end of the Command phase, the player whose turn it is scores 1VP for each objective marker they control (to a maximum of 4VP).

In the fifth battle round, the player who has the second turn scores VP as described above, but does so at the end of the turn instead of at the end of their Command phase.

Central Strongpoint — End Game Objective

Seize this point to raise your flag of victory over the very heart of the fortress.

Condition VP

Attacker controls the Central objective marker at end of battle 3VP
