The Skeins Unravel

Nachmund Gauntlet — Heralds of Vengeance

Battle Report

Despoilers Victory 5 — 4
++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 820.M42 ++

The skeins of fate have unravelled—and the Aeldari found only death in their threads. The Killing Stone's assault was swift and merciless, overwhelming the defenders before their reinforcements could arrive. When the Wraithblades finally emerged from the webway, they found only corpses.

The slaughter was near-total. Rangers, Storm Guardians, Dark Reapers—all cut down. The Falcon was torn from the sky, its Fire Dragon passengers scattered. Even the mighty Wraithlord fell, its spirit stone dark. The Howling Banshees fought with desperate fury to the last, their war-screams echoing through the corridors even as they were dragged down. The Warlock's psychic defences shattered against the tide of corruption.

Yet the victory cost the Chaos forces dearly. Cultists by the score were fed into the meat grinder, their sacrifice buying time for the Legionaries to secure the strike beacons. The Raptors' ambushes claimed many, but two squads of Legionaries and the Dark Commune paid the ultimate price. The Despoilers now control the teleportation arrays. The war enters a darker phase.
