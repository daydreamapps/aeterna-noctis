STRATEGIC AUGURY CONFIRMED: Xenos stronghold located — Sub-sector Malakor, Grid Ref IX-Tertius. Intelligence circuits burn with the revelation: the greenskin rabble have fortified a supply nexus within the hulk's corroded bowels, using its mineral abundance to forge crude weaponry and fuel their endless WAAAGH. From this festering cache they have launched strike after strike against the Omnissiah's servants.

Magos Prime has issued the Edict of Sacred Dismantling. The forge-blessed warriors of The Bottomless Bucket advance not in wrath, but in holy purpose — each supply cache destroyed is a prayer to the Machine God, each objective sabotaged a rite of purification. The greenskins believe their stronghold impregnable. They have not reckoned with the calculus of the Mechanicus.

The cogitators model a 94.7% probability of successful infiltration. The remaining 5.3% accounts for variables the machine-spirits refuse to quantify. The Orks, they note, do not behave as predicted.

"Data is the purest form of faith. Destroy what cannot be quantified."
— Magos Prime, pre-battle binharic broadcast

Mission Rules

Mission: Saboteurs 2 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Mission Rule — Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Mission Rule — Supply Sabotage (Action)

Starts: Your Shooting phase.
Units: One or more units from your Crusade army, each within range of a different objective marker.
Completes: End of your opponent's next turn (or end of battle), if you still control that objective marker.
If Completed: That objective marker is sabotaged and removed from the battlefield.

Victor Bonus

The victor's Crusade force gains 1 Requisition Point and 1 Strategic Asset Point.

Scoring Reference

Safeguard Supplies (Defender — Progressive)

From the second battle round onwards, at the end of the Defender's turn:

  • Control 1 objective marker → 1VP
  • Control 2–3 objective markers → 2VP
  • Control 4–5 objective markers → 3VP

Supplies Destroyed (Attacker — Progressive)

Each time the Attacker sabotages an objective marker:

  • Each sabotaged objective → 3VP
  • If 2 or more sabotaged in the same turn → +1VP bonus
