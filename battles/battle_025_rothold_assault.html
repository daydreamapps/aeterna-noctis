AFTER-ACTION REPORT: The Gauntlet engagement at the Rothold concluded. Outcome: INCONCLUSIVE. The Sons of Decay drove the assault; the Crusade of Binding Chains refused to yield the gate. Neither rot nor faith claimed dominion. Eleven dead on each side. The Rothold holds.

The Death Guard advance pressed the gauntlet hard from the opening turn — Plague Marines feeding the corridor with bodies and bolter-fire while the Foetid Bloat-Drone ranged the flanks. The Emperor's Champion met the Lord of Virulence in the centre and put him down; the Foul Blightspawn answered in the third round, drowning the Champion in concentrated bile. High Marshal Helbrecht held the line throughout, his presence calling down Mortal Wounds and rallying the wavering. Reinforcement waves on both sides absorbed every assault — Templar Crusaders against Death Guard recovery protocols, neither breaking, neither breaking through.

The Crusader Squad ran the gauntlet further than any other, accounting for four kills across Deathshroud, Plague Marines, and the Plague Lord Sepharox, before the Foetid Bloat-Drone ended them in the fifth round. That same Bloat-Drone — three kills to its name — was finally silenced by a lone Invader ATV in the dying moments. The Tallyman recorded everything from the backfield: untouched, patient, a scholar of attrition. The score immovable.

"I have memorised the face of every soul that fell today. Their names will be recited at the next liturgy."
— The Tallyman, 839.M42
