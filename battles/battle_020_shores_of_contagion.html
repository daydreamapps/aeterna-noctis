PRE-ENGAGEMENT BRIEFING: Sector Rho-Vitalis, the hulk's lower manufactorum ring. Threat classification: CONTAGION INCURSION. Biological hazard rating: EXTREME.

The Sons of Decay have come again. Twice the Adeptus Mechanicus of the Bottomless Bucket have barred their advance — twice the plague-swollen dead have risen to swallow those gains whole. Now the Death Guard press deeper, seeking not merely to contest ground but to establish an irrevocable beachhead from which no purging flame could ever fully cleanse them.

Arch-Magos Tertius Vorel has sealed Sector Rho-Vitalis behind quarantine bulkheads seventeen layers deep. His skitarii hold every choke point in overlapping fields of galvanic fire. He has run the probability cascades eight hundred times. The hulk's geometry favours the defender. The data is unambiguous.

And yet. The data did not account for the patience of the plague. The Sons of Decay do not assault — they arrive. They are already in the sector, already breathing their contagion into the recycled air. The bulkheads seal nothing that has not already been compromised. The beach was lost before the first shot fired.

"A beachhead is not taken by force. It is taken by rot."
— Recovered inscription, Sector Rho-Vitalis filtration chamber

Mission Overview

Mission: Beachhead Offensive 4 — Nachmund Gauntlet Crusade Mission

Charged with securing the toe-hold from which a more comprehensive invasion can be launched, one commander masses all their warriors and unleashes a desperate assault. Their opponent seeks to hurl them back, defending their territory in the process.

Mission Rule — Beachhead

In the Deploy Crusade Armies step, instead of the players alternating setting up their units, first the Attacker deploys all the units in their Primary Wave wholly within their deployment zone, and then the Defender sets up all of the units in their Primary Wave wholly within their deployment zone.

Mission Rule — Strategic Offensive

The Attacker decides which player has the first turn.

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one additional unit from their Crusade army to be Marked for Greatness.

Mission Objectives

Secure Beachhead — Progressive Objective

With every yard advanced into enemy territory, the invaders come closer to securing their beachhead.

From the second battle round onwards, at the end of the Command phase, the player whose turn it is scores VP as follows:

  • For each objective marker that the player controls, they score 1VP.
  • If it is the Attacker's turn and the Attacker controls the objective marker in their own deployment zone, then for each other objective marker they control, the Attacker scores an additional 1VP.
In the fifth battle round, the player who has the second turn scores VP as described above, but does so at the end of the turn instead of at the end of their Command phase.
