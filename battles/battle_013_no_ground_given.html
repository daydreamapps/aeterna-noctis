AFTER-ACTION REPORT: The Crusade of Binding Chains has secured Sector Kappa-7. Casualties sustained but objectives achieved. The Killing Stone forces have withdrawn under withering fire.

The Emperor's Champion, Sigismund's Blade, earned immortal glory this day. Beset by eleven Chaos Legionaries and thirteen Cultists at the central nexus, he carved a path of righteous slaughter through the heretic tide. The Aspiring Champion fell first, followed by five Legionaries, the Demagogue, and two cultists before Sigismund's Blade was finally overwhelmed—standing defiant atop a mountain of the slain.

The Breakers of Dawn and The Burning Brand distinguished themselves with exceptional valor. Marshal Grimvow has marked both units for greatness. Silent Judgement sustained critical damage and requires extensive reconstruction—the Neophyte now bears the mark of battle-weariness.

Strategic dominance achieved. The heretics gave ground. We did not.

Heroic Moment

The Emperor's Champion — Last Stand at the Nexus

Sigismund's Blade stands up to his name...

Beset by eleven Chaos Legionaries and thirteen Cultists at the central nexus, the Emperor's Champion carved through the enemy with righteous fury. The Aspiring Champion fell first beneath his Black Sword, followed by five Legionaries. The Demagogue of the Dark Commune was cut down mid-incantation, and two more cultists joined the pile of corpses.

Only when overwhelmed by sheer numbers did Sigismund's Blade finally fall—standing defiant atop a mountain of the slain, having earned his virtue: FRONT-LINE CRUSADER (Scouts 6" & re-roll charges).

Mission Rules

Staging Post

Players only gain 1CP during their turn if they control the objective marker within their deployment zone.

Critical: If you do not control your deployment zone objective, you do not gain CP at the start of your Command phase. Protect your staging post!

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Ruthless Aggression

There is no time for complacency in this fight. Do not give the foe an inch!

At the end of your turn:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers that were controlled by your opponent at the start of the turn 1 VP
If one or more of those objective markers were within your opponent's deployment zone +1 VP

Progressive Objective: Domination

Establish swift strategic dominance to deny your foes the chance to consolidate their position.

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers 1 VP
Control more objective markers than your opponent 2 VP
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

Victor Bonus

The victor can select one additional unit from their Crusade army to be Marked for Greatness.

This means the victor may Mark for Greatness two units in total after this battle (the standard one plus the bonus).
