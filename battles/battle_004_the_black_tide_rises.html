The Black Tide Rises

Nachmund Gauntlet — Front-line Warfare

Battle Report

Guardians Victory 10 — 0
++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Aeterna Noctis Sensorium ++ ++ Timestamp: 819.M42 ++

The black tide has swept through the Aeldari defensive line. In a devastating display of coordinated aggression, the Crusade of Binding Chains achieved total battlefield dominance, denying the xenos any foothold on the contested objectives.

The Breakers of Dawn eliminated the Ranger screening force in the opening salvoes. When a Falcon gunship materialized from the webway with Fire Dragons aboard, the Anvil Eternal responded with devastating firepower, destroying both craft and passengers in a single fusillade. The Chainbreakers carved through the Skyreavers while the Pyre of Heretics reduced the towering Wraithlord to scattered wraithbone.

Only Brother Aldric, the Emperor's Champion, fell to the alien constructs—struck down by the Wraithlord moments before its destruction. Yet even this loss could not dim the victory: Silent Judgement stood defiant against the construct's fury and survived to claim the kill. In the final moments, the Aeldari's desperate charges faltered and failed, their spirit broken.
