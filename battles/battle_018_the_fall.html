AFTER-ACTION REPORT — ENGAGEMENT: VITAL STRONGHOLD. The Bahzhakhain Süirel have been expunged from the contested sector. No Aeldari unit escaped the pestilential embrace of the Sons of Decay. The Hulk festers deeper.

The Plague Lord bearing the Crucible shard — designated SPURT GUSHER — prosecuted the xenos with singular ferocity. Fire Dragons, Swooping Hawks, and Storm Guardians fell to his corroded blade before any could retreat. He earned the mark of greatness in blood and contagion. The Lord of Virulence, ever remorseless, secured the stronghold's flank and was likewise elevated.

Of note: a Foetid Bloat-Drone, badly mauled across three cycles of fire by the Aeldari Falcon, their Ghost Warrior Crucible-champion, and the Fire Dragon shrines, refused annihilation. Sustained on two remaining hull points, it held position while the plague marines on its left were consumed. The machine spirit of Nurgle does not yield to mere firepower.

The Ghost Warrior Crucible-bearer, despite fighting with distinction, was destroyed — yet earned the mark of greatness in defeat, a testament to the xenos' grim resolve to record glory even in utter ruin.

Casualties for the Sons of Decay: one Chaos Rhino reduced to scrap iron, one Plague Marine squad ground to nothing. Tolerable tithe. The stronghold holds. The contagion spreads.

"They brought fire. We brought eternity."
— ScoutMaster the Pale, Post-Engagement Communion
