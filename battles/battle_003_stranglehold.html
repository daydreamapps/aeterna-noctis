Hulk Battle 003

Chokepoint at Lambda-3

Nachmund Gauntlet Mission

++ Priority: Crimson ++ ++ Source: Forward Observation Post Lambda-3 ++ ++ Clearance: Warmaster Eyes Only ++

Chaotic drop assault confirmed in Sector Lambda-3. Multiple insertion points detected. Both Imperial and traitor forces scattered across the engagement zone. First to consolidate will control the sector.

The Crusade of Binding Chains has deployed. High Marshal Aldric Grimvow leads his Templars into the breach once more. Fresh from their victory at Aethelgard—where they annihilated the Sons of Decay to the last plague-bearer—they seek to extend the Guardians' dominion deeper into the Hulk. The Chapel of Eternal Vigil still awaits a new Emperor's Champion, but the crusaders' faith burns no less bright.

But the Ruinous Powers have answered. Chaos Space Marines pour from the warp-tainted corridors, their allegiances unknown, their hatred absolute. Eight-pointed stars adorn their corrupted armour. They seek not just victory—they seek to break the Imperium's grip on this cursed vessel.

In the wake of scattered deployment, whoever seizes the staging posts first will strangle their enemy's advance.

Mission Concept: In the wake of a chaotic drop assault, invaders and defenders alike are scattered. The first commander to secure a decisive and commanding position on this battlefield will be able to drive their foes before them and consolidate their strength.

Deployment Zones

Mission Rules

Staging Post

CP Restriction: Players only gain 1 CP during their turn if they control the objective marker within their own deployment zone.

If you do not control your deployment zone objective, you do not gain CP that turn. Secure your staging post or be starved of resources!

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Ruthless Aggression

At the end of your turn:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers that were controlled by your opponent at the start of the turn 1 VP
Bonus: If one or more of those objectives were in opponent's deployment zone +1 VP
Key: You score for taking objectives from your opponent—not just holding them. Aggression is rewarded!

Progressive Objective: Domination

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your Command phase:

Condition VP
Control one or more objective markers 1 VP
Control more objective markers than your opponent 2 VP
Round 5 Exception: The player with the second turn scores at the end of their turn instead of their Command phase.

Army Setup: Tactical Reserves

Nachmund Gauntlet uses Tactical Reserves — your army is split into waves that arrive throughout the battle.

Incursion (1000pts) Wave Configuration

Wave Points Limit Arrives
Primary Wave Up to 400 pts Deployment (start of battle)
Reinforcement Wave 1 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 2
Reinforcement Wave 2 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 3
Your total army can exceed 1000pts — the wave limits are separate. Plan your Order of Battle accordingly.

Building Your Waves

  • Assign each unit to a wave before the battle
  • Reinforcement waves arrive from your battlefield edge (or via Deep Strike if the unit has that ability)
  • You choose which reinforcement wave arrives at the start of each round

Agenda Selection

Before the battle, select 2 Agendas for your army. Units can earn XP by completing Agenda objectives.

Option 1: Nachmund Agendas

Role-specific agendas. Choose based on whether you are Attacker or Defender.

Defender Agendas (3)

Activate Defence Perimeter

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in your deployment zone performs "Activate Shield Node" (Shooting phase). Completes end of opponent's next turn.

+1 XP per node

+1 SAP if 4+ nodes

Repel the Foe

Destroy enemy Reinforcement wave units.

+1 XP per kill (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if entire wave wiped

Defiant to the End

Select up to 3 units at battle start.

+2 XP if survives, +1 XP if above half

+1 SAP if any at Starting Strength

Attacker Agendas (3)

Symbolic Objectives

Opponent selects 2 objectives. Control them, select up to 3 units within range at end.

+2 XP per selected unit

+1 SAP if control all selected

Prime Macro-Ordnance

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in enemy deployment zone performs "Prime Planetquake Bomb" (Shooting phase).

+2 XP per completion (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if completed 2+ times

Raze and Ruin

Destroy enemies near objectives.

+1 XP per kill (+2 XP if enemy zone). Max 3 XP/unit

+1 SAP if control all enemy objectives

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

Final Score Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
Kills Which of your units destroyed which enemy units (for "Dealers of Death" XP: +1 XP per 3 units destroyed)
Casualties Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
XP Earned Per unit: +1 XP (participation) + Agenda XP + Dealers of Death + Marked for Greatness (if applicable)
Notable Moments 2-3 memorable events for the battle report (heroic last stands, dramatic kills, lucky/unlucky dice, etc.)
