Hulk Battle 002

Heralds of Vengeance

Nachmund Gauntlet Mission

++ Priority: Amber ++ ++ Source: Sensorium Array Kappa-12 ++ ++ Clearance: Tactical Command ++

Warning—anomalous energy signatures detected in Sector Kappa-12. Readings consistent with mass teleport beacon calibration AND necrodermis reanimation protocols. Two hostile forces converge on the same junction.

The Sons of Decay have returned. Das Boot, the Lord of Poxes who fell at Aethelgard, walks again—Grandfather Nurgle's gifts are not so easily refused. His plague-touched warriors seek to establish strike beacons for a devastating orbital bombardment that will spread Nurgle's blessings across the Hulk.

But something older stirs in the darkness. The Tekhsarun Awakening has begun. Necron warriors, dormant for sixty million years, have risen from their tomb-crypts within the Hulk's ancient superstructure. Their Overlord calculates that these corridors belong to the Dynasty—and the living are merely trespassers to be removed.

Both forces race to guide strike beacons into enemy territory. Whoever triangulates their targeting solution first will unleash devastation upon their foes.

Mission Overview

Board Size 60" x 44"
Objectives 3 Total
Deployment Diagonal
Victor Bonus +1 RP, +1 SAP
Mission Concept: Ominous threats stand ready to be unleashed—mass teleport strikes, fleet bombardment, or malevolent ritual super weapons. These war-changing assets must be guided by hand, requiring bold warriors to serve as their heralds.

Deployment Zones

Objective Placement

  • 1 objective in the centre of the battlefield
  • 2 objectives placed 20" from centre, 8" from deployment zone edges (as shown)

Mission Rules

Guide Strike Beacon (Action)

Starts Your Shooting phase
Units One or more units from your Crusade army, each within range of a different objective marker that you control
Completes End of your turn
If Completed: For each unit that completes this Action, you can move the objective marker that unit is within range of up to 6". The objective marker cannot end that move on top of any other objective marker or model.

Scoring

Progressive Objective: Strike Triangulation Achieved

From Round 2 onwards, at the end of your turn:

Objective Position VP
Wholly within opponent's deployment zone 3 VP
Wholly within 6" of opponent's deployment zone 2 VP
Wholly within 12" of opponent's deployment zone 1 VP
Note: These VP values are scored per objective marker in that position. VP values are not cumulative—each objective scores based on its closest qualifying position only.

Army Setup: Tactical Reserves

Nachmund Gauntlet uses Tactical Reserves — your army is split into waves that arrive throughout the battle.

Incursion (1000pts) Wave Configuration

Wave Points Limit Arrives
Primary Wave Up to 400 pts Deployment (start of battle)
Reinforcement Wave 1 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 2
Reinforcement Wave 2 Up to 300 pts Start of Battle Round 3
Your total army can exceed 1000pts — the wave limits are separate. Plan your Order of Battle accordingly.

Building Your Waves

  • Assign each unit to a wave before the battle
  • Reinforcement waves arrive from your battlefield edge (or via Deep Strike if the unit has that ability)
  • You choose which reinforcement wave arrives at the start of each round

Agenda Selection

Before the battle, select 2 Agendas for your army. Units can earn XP by completing Agenda objectives.

Option 1: Nachmund Agendas

Role-specific agendas. Choose based on whether you are Attacker or Defender.

Defender Agendas (3)

Activate Defence Perimeter

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in your deployment zone performs "Activate Shield Node" (Shooting phase). Completes end of opponent's next turn.

+1 XP per node

+1 SAP if 4+ nodes

Repel the Foe

Destroy enemy Reinforcement wave units.

+1 XP per kill (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if entire wave wiped

Defiant to the End

Select up to 3 units at battle start.

+2 XP if survives, +1 XP if above half

+1 SAP if any at Starting Strength

Attacker Agendas (3)

Symbolic Objectives

Opponent selects 2 objectives. Control them, select up to 3 units within range at end.

+2 XP per selected unit

+1 SAP if control all selected

Prime Macro-Ordnance

Action: INFANTRY/MOUNTED in enemy deployment zone performs "Prime Planetquake Bomb" (Shooting phase).

+2 XP per completion (max 3 XP/unit)

+1 SAP if completed 2+ times

Raze and Ruin

Destroy enemies near objectives.

+1 XP per kill (+2 XP if enemy zone). Max 3 XP/unit

+1 SAP if control all enemy objectives

Post-Battle Reporting

After the battle, report the following to your Game Master:

Final Score Your VP total and your opponent's VP total
Kills Which of your units destroyed which enemy units (for "Dealers of Death" XP: +1 XP per 3 units destroyed)
Casualties Which of your units were destroyed (they must make Out of Action tests)
XP Earned Per unit: +1 XP (participation) + Agenda XP + Dealers of Death + Marked for Greatness (if applicable)
Notable Moments 2-3 memorable events for the battle report (heroic last stands, dramatic kills, lucky/unlucky dice, etc.)
