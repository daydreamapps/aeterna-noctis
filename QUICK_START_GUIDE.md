# Aeterna Noctis - Player Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Crusade! This guide covers everything you need to join the fight for the Malakor Sector.

---

## What is Crusade?

Crusade is a narrative campaign where your army grows over time. Units gain experience, earn Battle Honours (upgrades), and sometimes suffer Battle Scars (penalties). Your victories matter, your losses have consequences, and your heroes can become legends.

---

## The Story So Far

The Space Hulk *Aeterna Noctis* has entered the Malakor Sector. Its massive warp-wake has anchored six worlds, dragging them toward the system's sun. Three alliances now fight for control:

- **The Guardians** - Those who seek to save the sector
- **The Despoilers** - Those who would see it burn
- **The Marauders** - Those who care only for plunder

You must choose your side.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Choose Your Alliance
Coordinate with the campaign organiser to join one of the three alliances.

### Step 2: Build Your Order of Battle
Select units for your starting roster. This campaign uses an **escalation format**:

| Phase | Battle Size | Points |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Phase 1 | Combat Patrol / Breach | 500 pts |
| Phase 2 | Incursion / Interdiction | 1000 pts |
| Phase 3 | Strike Force / Hulk | 2000 pts |

Start with a 500pt roster. You'll expand as the campaign progresses.

### Step 3: Claim a Home World
Each alliance controls certain planets. Your Home World grants you a **Boon** - but it can be raided and disabled by enemies.

| World | Boon | Penalty if Disabled |
|-------|------|---------------------|
| Ocularis Prime | Swap Agendas after seeing opponent's | No Surgical Deep Strikes |
| Ferrum IX | Remove Battle Scars for 0 RP | Vehicles/Monsters gain no XP |
| Veridian Reach | +1 to Out of Action tests | -1 to Advance/Charge |
| Sanctum Malakor | +1 Starting CP | No Faction Stratagems |
| Aethelgard | Free "Fresh Recruits" per phase | No unit reinforcement |
| Void-Spire 7 | +1 SAP per Hulk victory | No Artificer Relics |

2. Create an account (free)

## The Three Battle Types

### The Breach (Phase 0 Skirmishes)
- **Size:** 500 pts
- **Purpose:** Earn Intel Points for your alliance
- **Stakes:** Low - good for learning and warming up

### The Interdiction (Raids)
- **Size:** 500-1000 pts
- **Rules:** Lair of the Tyrant (Maelstrom)
- **Purpose:** Raid enemy Home Worlds to disable their Boons, or steal RP
- **Warning:** Raiding means you skip the Hulk battle that round

### The Hulk (Main Battles)
- **Size:** 1000-2000 pts
- **Rules:** Nachmund Gauntlet (modified)
- **Purpose:** Control Strategic Sites, earn Campaign Victory Points (CVP)
- **Sites:** The Bridge, The Spires, The Relay, The Hangar

## Crusade Mechanics - The Essentials

### Requisition Points (RP)
- **Start with:** 5 RP
- **Gain:** 1 RP after each battle

| Requisition | Cost | Effect |
|-------------|------|--------|
| Fresh Recruits | 1 RP | Add a new unit to your roster |
| Increase Supply Limit | 1 RP | +100 pts to your Supply Limit |
| Repair and Recuperate | 1 RP | Remove one Battle Scar |
| Rearm and Resupply | 1 RP | Change a unit's wargear |

### Experience Points (XP)
Units earn XP each battle:
- Destroyed an enemy unit: **+1 XP**
- Performed an action: **+1 XP**
- Marked for Greatness (1 unit per battle, your choice): **+3 XP**

### Ranks and Battle Honours
As units gain XP, they rank up and earn upgrades:

| XP | Rank | Battle Honours |
|----|------|----------------|
| 0-5 | Battle-ready | 0 |
| 6-15 | Blooded | 1 |
| 16-30 | Battle-hardened | 2 |
| 31-50 | Heroic | 3 |
| 51+ | Legendary | 4 |

### Battle Scars
When a unit is destroyed, roll D6. On a **1**, it gains a Battle Scar (a permanent penalty). Remove scars by spending 1 RP or using Ferrum IX's boon.

**Danger:** Units with more Scars than Honours are **Out of Action** until healed.

---

## After Each Battle

Use this checklist:

```
[ ] Gain 1 RP
[ ] Award XP to participating units
[ ] Mark one unit for Greatness (+3 XP)
[ ] Roll for Battle Scars (D6 per destroyed unit, scar on 1)
[ ] Check for rank-ups and select Battle Honours
[ ] Spend RP if desired
[ ] Update Administratum
[ ] Report results to campaign organiser
```

## Campaign Special Rules

### The Sun Timer
As phases progress, the worlds drift closer to the sun. Environmental hazards intensify:

| Phase | Hazard |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | None |
| 2 | Solar Flares (D3 MW per unit on 5+) |
| 3 | Warp Instability (Psychic -1, Perils on doubles) |
| 4 | Gravitational Stress (No Fall Back) |
| 5 | The Dying Light (Night Fighting, 18" visibility) |

### The Betrayal Clause
If you abandon a Hulk battle to raid a planet instead, your alliance automatically records a **Loss** in the Nachmund track.

## Submitting Battle Reports

After each game, report the following to the campaign organiser:

1. **Battle Type:** Breach / Interdiction / Hulk
2. **Players and Alliances**
3. **Points Level**
4. **Mission Played**
5. **Result** (Victory/Defeat/Draw and score)
6. **Units destroyed** (both sides)
7. **Marked for Greatness:** Which unit?
8. **Battle Scar rolls:** Any 1s?
9. **Narrative notes:** Anything memorable?
