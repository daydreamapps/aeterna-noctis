## The Bridge
**Function:** Command and navigation systems

*The Bridge of the Aeterna Noctis is a cathedral of corroded brass and flickering hololith displays. Whoever controls it may yet steer this abomination—if the machine spirits can be placated.*

**Strategic Site Bonus:** While your alliance controls this site, each time a player in that alliance musters a Crusade army, they can muster up to 4 Reinforcement waves instead of up to 3. This does not let players deploy more Reinforcement waves during the battle, but gives them greater tactical flexibility.

## The Spires
**Function:** Sensor arrays and communications

*Rising from the Hulk's twisted superstructure, the Spires house ancient auspex arrays capable of tracking every ship in the sector—and perhaps beyond.*

**Strategic Site Bonus:** While your alliance controls this site, when a player in that alliance plays a game, each time an enemy unit is set up from Strategic Reserves, roll one D6: on a 5+, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.

## The Relay
**Function:** Warp engine stabilizers

*The Relay pulses with sickly light, its systems barely containing the warp energies that propel the Hulk. To control it is to control the Hulk's fate—and the sector's.*

**Strategic Site Bonus:** While your alliance controls this site, your alliance can select one additional Strategic Goal at the start of the campaign phase. At the end of the campaign phase, before any Strategic Goals rewards are awarded, your alliance must discard one of its Strategic Goals. Warmaster coordinates.

## The Hangar
**Function:** Launch bays and docking access

*The Hangar yawns open to the void, its mag-clamps and launch rails still functional despite millennia of neglect. From here, strike forces deploy to the anchored worlds.*

**Strategic Site Bonus:** While your alliance controls this site, players in that alliance can perform up to two Surgical Deep Strikes per turn. In addition, once per turn, when a unit in that player's army performs a Surgical Deep Strike, that player can re-roll their unit's Deep Strike test.
