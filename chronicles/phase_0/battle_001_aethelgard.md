# Battle 001: The Defence of Aethelgard

**Date:** 814.M42 (March 14, 2026)
**Location:** Aethelgard, Knight World
**Battle Type:** Side-Skirmish (Planetary Defence)
**Points:** 1000

---

## Combatants

**Defenders:** The Crusade of Binding Chains (Black Templars)
**Attackers:** The Sons of Decay (Death Guard)

---

## Outcome

**VICTORY: The Crusade of Binding Chains**
**Final Score:** 88 - 28

The Death Guard assault force was annihilated to the last plague-bearer. Not a single corrupted Astartes escaped the killing fields of Aethelgard. However, victory came at a terrible cost—the Emperor's Champion fell in single combat, his sacrifice buying the time needed to complete the purge.

+++BEGIN TRANSCRIPT+++

*The knight world of Aethelgard burns no more.*

*When the plague ships made planetfall, the Crusade of Binding Chains stood ready. High Marshal Aldric Grimvow had read the warp-signs. He knew they would come.*

*What followed was not a battle—it was an execution.*

*The Sons of Decay advanced through fields of fire that would have shattered lesser hosts. Their Bloat-drone Geoff vomited caustic filth across the Templar lines. Poxwalkers shambled forward in rotting tides. Yet the Black Templars held. The Crusader Squads formed an unbreakable wall of ceramite and faith.*

*Then HE stepped forward.*

*The Emperor's Champion strode into the enemy host. The Black Sword sang as it carved through corrupted flesh. He duelled their Lord of Poxes, Das Boot, blade against plague-rusted axe. Though the creature's foulness clawed at his soul, he would not yield.*

*In the end, both fell.*

*The Champion struck the killing blow, driving the Black Sword through Das Boot's secondary heart even as daemon-plague consumed him from within. He spoke no final words. The litany he had recited for nine hours was complete.*

*By dusk, not a single Death Guard remained standing. In the Chapel of Eternal Vigil, every battle-brother kneels in silent prayer, waiting for a new Champion to be chosen.*

*The chains of duty remain unbroken.*

+++END TRANSCRIPT+++

## Campaign Effects

- **Aethelgard:** The Guardians gain planetary control. Defences reinforced.
- **The Crusade of Binding Chains:** +1 RP (victory bonus). Surviving units gain 1 XP each.
- **The Emperor's Champion:** KILLED IN ACTION. Awaiting new Champion selection.
- **The Sons of Decay:** No RP gained. All units destroyed—no XP earned. Casualty recovery rolls required.
