# Battle 001: The Defence of Aethelgard

**Date:** 814.M42 (March 14, 2026)
**Location:** Aethelgard, Knight World
**Battle Type:** Side-Skirmish (Planetary Defence)
**Points:** 1000

---

## Combatants

**Defenders:** The Crusade of Binding Chains (Black Templars)
**Attackers:** The Sons of Decay (Death Guard)

---

## Outcome

**VICTORY: The Crusade of Binding Chains**
**Final Score:** 88 - 28

The Death Guard assault force was annihilated to the last plague-bearer. Not a single corrupted Astartes escaped the killing fields of Aethelgard. However, victory came at a terrible cost—the Emperor's Champion fell in single combat, his sacrifice buying the time needed to complete the purge.

+++BEGIN TRANSCRIPT+++

*The knight world of Aethelgard burns no more.*

*When the plague ships of the Death Guard made planetfall at dawn, the defenders of the Crusade of Binding Chains stood ready upon the ramparts of Fortress Ironhollow. High Marshal Aldric Grimvow had read the warp-signs. He knew they would come.*

*What followed was not a battle—it was an execution.*

*The Sons of Decay, those wretched servants of the Plaguefather, advanced through fields of fire that would have shattered lesser hosts. Their Bloat-drone Geoff vomited streams of caustic filth across the Templar lines. Poxwalkers shambled forward in endless, rotting tides. The Deathshroud cut down everything before them with scythes dripping with corruption.*

*Yet the Black Templars held.*

*The Crusader Squads—the Anvil Eternal and the Pyre of Heretics—formed an unbreakable wall of ceramite and faith. The Storm Speeder Wrath Incarnate screamed overhead, its missiles detonating ammunition stores and scattering the traitors' formations. When the line buckled, Captain Hektor the Unbroken was there, his power fist crushing the bloated helm of a Plague Marine champion.*

*Then HE stepped forward.*

*The Emperor's Champion, the Anointed Blade, strode into the heart of the enemy host. The Black Sword sang as it carved through corrupted flesh, each strike a prayer made manifest. He duelled their Lord of Poxes, Das Boot, blade against plague-rusted axe, and though the creature's foulness clawed at his soul, he would not yield.*

*In the end, both fell.*

*The Emperor's Champion struck the killing blow, driving the Black Sword through Das Boot's secondary heart even as the daemon-plague consumed his own body from within. He spoke no final words. He did not need to. The litany he had recited for nine hours aboard the Sigismund's Wrath was complete.*

*By dusk, not a single Death Guard remained standing upon Aethelgard. The planet's defences are being reinforced. The Knight Houses have pledged their support. And in the Chapel of Eternal Vigil, every battle-brother of the Crusade of Binding Chains kneels in silent prayer, waiting for the visions to come again.*

*Waiting for a new Champion to be chosen.*

*The chains of duty remain unbroken.*

+++END TRANSCRIPT+++

## Campaign Effects

- **Aethelgard:** The Guardians gain planetary control. Defences reinforced.
- **The Crusade of Binding Chains:** +1 RP (victory bonus). Surviving units gain 1 XP each.
- **The Emperor's Champion:** KILLED IN ACTION. Awaiting new Champion selection.
- **The Sons of Decay:** No RP gained. All units destroyed—no XP earned. Casualty recovery rolls required.
