## THE ANCHORED WORLDS BURN

The war began not aboard the Hulk, but on the planets it drags toward oblivion.

On **Aethelgard**, the Death Guard made planetfall with plague and purpose. The Sons of Decay sought to corrupt the knight world, but the Crusade of Binding Chains stood ready. What followed was a massacre—but not the one Nurgle's children intended. The Black Templars slaughtered every last plague-bearer, though the Emperor's Champion fell in single combat against the Lord of Poxes, Das Boot. Both warriors struck killing blows. Both fell. *The chains of duty remain unbroken.*

At **Sanctum Malakor**, the Death Guard found easier prey. The Aeldari of Bahzhakhain Süirel, exiles of Iyanden, attempted to contest the cardinal world's supply lines. The Deathshroud Terminators materialised in their midst like executioners stepping from nightmare. By battle's end, not a single xenos drew breath. *Grandfather Nurgle's gifts spread across the sacred soil.*

---

## THE HULK AWAKENS

With the anchored worlds contested, all eyes turned to the Aeterna Noctis itself.

**Sector Theta-9** saw first blood. The Crusade of Binding Chains breached the Hulk's outer hull, only to find the Trans-Eternians Alliance—Votann prospectors seeking ancient archaeotech—already within. The battle was brutal and close-fought, but the Black Templars prevailed. *The Guardians claim first victory aboard the Hulk.*

In **Sector Kappa-12**, the Sons of Decay sought redemption. They found only ancient death. The Tekhsarun Awakening—Necrons stirred from aeons of slumber by the Hulk's warp-wake—descended upon the Death Guard with implacable fury. Gauss flayers flensed ceramite. Reanimation protocols mocked every hard-won kill. The Despoilers were routed. *The Marauders announce their presence with authority.*

The tide turned at **Sector Lambda-3**. Chaos Space Marines—the vanguard of a larger force—caught the Crusade of Binding Chains at a chokepoint. Without their fallen Champion, the Black Templars fought with cold fury but could not break through. The Despoilers claimed the sector. *The war for the Hulk has truly begun.*

---

## THE KILLING STONE ARRIVES

As the week closes, augur-stations detect new signatures translating from the warp. The Killing Stone—a warband of Chaos Space Marines bearing iconography whispered in dread—has pledged itself to the Despoilers. Their Chaos Lord, Yubaba, speaks of dark visions and greater purposes.

The Despoilers grow stronger. The balance shifts.
