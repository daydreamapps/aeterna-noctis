# Battle 002: Skirmish at Sanctum Malakor

**Date:** 815.M42 (March 21, 2026)
**Location:** Sanctum Malakor, Cardinal World
**Battle Type:** Skirmish (Open Play)
**Points:** 1000
**Mission:** Supply Drop

---

## Combatants

**Attackers:** The Sons of Decay (Death Guard) — The Despoilers
**Defenders:** Bahzhakhain Süirel (Aeldari) — The Marauders

---

## Outcome

**VICTORY: The Sons of Decay**
**Final Score:** 65 - 21

The Death Guard assault force overwhelmed the Aeldari defenders on the outskirts of Sanctum Malakor. Despite early losses—including their Rhino being annihilated by Fire Dragons—the Sons of Decay's inexorable advance proved unstoppable. The Tri-Lobe Vectors and Deathshroud Terminators carved through the Aeldari lines, with not a single xenos unit left standing by battle's end.

*The cathedral spires of Sanctum Malakor cast long shadows across the battlefield as the forces clashed.*

*The Aeldari of Iyanden struck first. Their Fire Dragons burst from a swooping Falcon, fusion guns blazing, reducing the Chaos Rhino to molten slag in moments. The Wraithlord and Falcon combined their firepower to eliminate the Chaos Spawn and tear through the shambling Poxwalker hordes.*

*But the Death Guard do not break. They do not retreat. They only advance.*

*The Deathshroud Terminators materialised in the heart of the Aeldari formation, their scythes reaping a terrible toll. Rangers fell. Aspect Warriors were cut down. The Wraithblades fought desperately, a lone Howling Banshee Exarch managing to fell three of the Plague-touched Terminators before being overwhelmed.*

*The Lord of Poxes, Das Boot—returned from death itself after Aethelgard—strode into the fray. The Storm Guardians who had seized the central objective could not withstand his might. The Tri-Lobe Vectors, those champions of Nurgle's Crucible, carved through the Wraithlord and turned their attention to the remaining Aeldari armour.*

*By the fifth hour, the Falcon was a burning wreck. The Dark Reapers were scattered bones. The Warlock's psychic screams echoed across the vox before falling silent.*

*Grandfather Nurgle's gifts spread across Sanctum Malakor.*

## Campaign Effects

- **Sanctum Malakor:** The Despoilers gain minor planetary control.
- **The Sons of Decay:** +1 RP (victory bonus). Units gain 1 XP each.
- **Bahzhakhain Süirel:** No RP gained. Units gain 1 XP each.

---

## Battle Summary

### Round 1
- **Death Guard:** Established centre objective (2 VP). Holding No Prisoners.
- **Aeldari:** Fire Dragons destroyed the Rhino. Falcon and Wraithlord eliminated Chaos Spawn and Poxwalkers. Engaged on all fronts (2 VP).

### Round 2
- **Death Guard:** No Prisoners (4 VP), Extend Battlelines (4 VP). Deathshroud deep-struck into centre, devastated Rangers and Aspect Warriors, engaged Wraithlord and Wraithblades.
- **Aeldari:** Corsair Skyreavers deep-struck to contest. Storm Guardians and Wraithblade/Banshee Exarch killed 3 Deathshroud. Tempting Target (5 VP).

### Round 3
- **Death Guard:** 5 VP primary (Omega objective). Lord of Poxes reinforced Tri-Lobes, Wraithlord destroyed, Storm Guardians crushed. Engage on all fronts (2 VP).
- **Aeldari:** Falcon pulse laser killed one Tri-Lobe. Dark Reapers and Warlock psychic attacks destroyed more Tri-Lobes. Extend Battlelines (4 VP).

### Round 4
- **Death Guard:** Tri-Lobes charged Falcon, Dark Reapers, and intervening Warlock. All three eliminated. Marked for Death (7 VP), Bring it Down (4 VP). **All Aeldari units destroyed.**
- **Aeldari:** Unable to score.

### Round 5
- **Death Guard:** 15 VP for Omega objective, Recover Assets (5 VP).
