Phase III

The Closing Labyrinth

Vox-Intercept // Priority Omega

Transmitted: Phase III Commences

It began without warning. At zero-four-hundred shipboard, the Aeterna Noctis accelerated — not by void-engine, for none function; not by Warp-tide, for the augurs sang no such song. The Hulk simply moved, as if remembering a course set ten thousand years ago. And as it moved, it began to close.

Bulkheads twenty metres thick slammed shut along corridors that had stood open since the Heresy. Whole transit shafts collapsed inward. Whole decks folded. Companies that marched as one were cleaved in two by descending blast-doors. Cohorts that held a junction at dusk woke at dawn to find that junction no longer existed — only a wall of black iron, humming with current that had no source.

The Hulk is herding us. Every sealed door, every collapsed gallery, every redirected vent of toxic vapour drives the survivors core-ward. Toward a chamber none of our charts marked. Toward an aperture that opened — opened, as if waiting — at the precise moment the outer exits sealed.

The core is exposed. The core is waiting. And the corridors between are now narrow enough that we may only meet each other.

Your forces are cut off. The units that were not at your side when the bulkheads fell are gone — pinned behind sealed iron, crushed beneath collapsing decks, lost to the dark. Their experience lives on in those who remain. Their banners do not.

Two cycles. Six battles. Three alliances. One opened heart. Bring only what you can carry. Bring only those who answered the muster. The Hulk is closing. Make the final blows count.

Phase III Rules

Format

  • Duration: 2 weeks.
  • 6 battles + 1 optional Grand Finale.
  • Alliance vs Alliance round-robin. Each pairing plays 2 battles.
  • Each alliance nominates two different players across the two battles of a given pairing.
  • Teams self-coordinate scheduling within the 2-week window.
  • No-show = battle recorded as missed (0 CVP).

Game

  • 10th Edition tournament rules.
  • 2,000 pts per side. 500 pt reserves cap.
  • Mission: Purge the Foe — primary scoring only, no secondaries.
  • No campaign waves, no Crusade sub-rules — clean tournament game.
  • Unit upgrades, traits, battle honours, Hulk bonuses, and Crusade Blessings (CP-difference based) all carry over.

Army Lists

  • Build up to 2,000 pts from your current Crusade roster.
  • Units not selected are destroyed in-narrative. Their XP may be reallocated onto surviving units (XP only — not traits/honours).
  • New units may be added as fresh additions.
  • Thematic upgrades permitted (e.g. Gravis → Terminators, transport → battle tank). Carry XP, pay new points cost. Keep it thematic.
  • Sacrifice + reallocate XP to debut or level up a chosen unit.

CVP & Finale

  • Win: 4 CVP   Draw: 2 CVP
  • Loss: 1 CVP   Missed: 0 CVP
  • Track survivors after each battle.
  • Grand Finale: three-way battle of surviving units. Narrative-first, optional.
