The Legend of Fabricator-Colonellus Harlandus Sarn-Dehr

Three thousand years ago, a Tech-Priest of peculiar habits departed Kentarchus Minoris aboard the exploration vessel The Colonel's Bounty. He wore robes of purest white—unstained by sacred oil through some lost protective coating technology. His rebreather was shaped into a pointed protrusion, almost like a goatee of brushed steel. He carried with him the Eleven Herbaceous Mysteries: STC fragments of legendary nutrient-optimization protocols that promised perfect efficiency in all machine operations.

His ship entered the space hulk designated Aeterna Noctis. Neither vessel nor Fabricator-Colonellus was ever seen again.

Now the hulk has returned. The expedition seeks the Sacred Receptacle—the cylindrical reliquary said to contain the Eleven Mysteries in their complete form. They call it The Receptacle Without End. They call it The Bucket.

The Bucket provides. The Bucket demands.
