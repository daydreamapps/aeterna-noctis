# The Crusade of Binding Chains

*"We are bound to duty. We are bound to war. We are bound to victory—or to death in His name."*

**Faction:** Adeptus Astartes — Black Templars
**Detachment:** Bastion Task Force
**Alliance:** The Guardians
**Home World:** Aethelgard
**Supply Limit:** 1400 pts
**Requisition Points:** 2

---

## High Command

### High Marshal Aldric Grimvow
**Marshal** | 80 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Wargear:* Plasma Pistol

> *"The Chains of Duty"*
>
> Aldric earned his epithet after the Siege of Kallos Tertius, where he refused evacuation despite seventeen confirmed wounds. He ordered his armour's joints welded shut so he could not be carried from the field. The crusade broke the heretic lines that day. Now he leads the Malakor Crusade with that same unyielding resolve, his oath of the moment etched into his gorget: *"Until the sun claims us, or we claim victory."*

---

### Captain Hektor the Unbroken
**Captain in Gravis Armour** | 90 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Wargear:* Boltstorm Gauntlet, Power Fist, Relic Chainsword
*Enhancements:* Artificer Armour

> *Gravis-Clad Warden of the Vanguard*
>
> Three times Hektor has been declared dead. Three times his hearts have started beating again. At Fornax Hive, a Chaos Knight stepped on him. At the Veldros Crossing, he held a plasma reactor breach with his bare gauntlets until reinforcements arrived. On the *Aeterna Noctis*, a corridor collapsed and buried him for six hours. Each time he emerges from the rubble, armour cracked but standing. The Neophytes whisper that the Emperor simply refuses to let him die. Hektor does not correct them.

---

### The Emperor's Champion
**Emperor's Champion (Anointed)** | 100 pts | **KILLED IN ACTION**

> *The Anointed Blade — Fallen at Aethelgard*
>
> He was Brother-Initiate Caden once. Now he is simply the vessel. When the visions came aboard the strike cruiser *Sigismund's Wrath*, he spoke the words of the Litany of Divine Guidance for nine hours without pause. When he finally fell silent, his eyes had changed—one black as the void, one burning gold. He does not speak of his former life. He does not need to. The Black Sword speaks for him.
>
> **FINAL TESTAMENT:** At Aethelgard, the Anointed Blade faced the Lord of Poxes Das Boot in single combat. Though the daemon-plague clawed at his soul, he drove the Black Sword through the traitor's secondary heart. Both fell. The litany was complete. The Crusade now awaits the visions that will reveal his successor.

---

### Apothecary Mordecai Sternhand
**Apothecary** | 50 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)

> *Keeper of the Sacred Vitae*
>
> Mordecai has served four Marshals and extracted gene-seed from over two thousand fallen brothers. His left hand is a bionic replacement—the original was lost reaching into a dying brother's chest cavity as a Tyranid bio-acid ate through his gauntlet. He completed the extraction. He always completes the extraction. The crusade's legacy flows through his reductor, and he guards it with a ferocity that rivals any Sword Brother.

## Battleline

### The Breakers of Dawn
**Assault Intercessor Squad (5)** | 75 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Led by Sword Brother Konrad Ashwrath*

> *Sword-Brothers of the First Strike*
>
> This squad has a single purpose: be the first through the breach. They take the Vow of the Furious Charge before every battle, binding their combat knives to their fists with iron chains. Konrad keeps a tally of every door, wall, and enemy line they have broken. The count stands at one hundred and forty-three. He intends to double it before the crusade ends.

---

### The Anvil Eternal
**Crusader Squad (10)** | 150 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Led by Initiate-Sergeant Baldur Ironjaw*

> *First Fighting Company, Veteran Initiates with Neophyte Charges*
>
> Baldur leads this squad of hardened Initiates and their eager Neophyte charges. The Neophytes call themselves "The Untempered"—raw iron waiting to be forged. Baldur tells them the same thing every battle: *"Watch. Learn. Survive. Then we talk."* The squad has not lost a Neophyte in seven engagements. Baldur intends to keep it that way.

---

### The Pyre of Heretics
**Crusader Squad (10)** | 150 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Led by Initiate-Sergeant Volker Brandtfist*

> *Second Fighting Company, Zealots of the Eternal Crusade*
>
> Where the Anvil holds the line, the Pyre advances. Volker demands his squad recite the Canticle of Hatred with every charge, their voices drowning out even bolter fire. The Neophytes assigned to this squad learn fury before they learn caution. Three of them have already earned their black carapace through acts of reckless valour. Volker approves.

### The Silent Judgement
**Intercessor Squad (5)** | 80 pts | Battle-ready (1 XP)
*Led by Brother-Initiate Erasmus Coldgrave*

> *Marksmen of the Marshal's Retinue*
>
> Not all Black Templars charge screaming into melee. Erasmus and his squad provide disciplined fire support, their bolt rifles delivering the Emperor's judgement at range. They speak little, even among themselves. Erasmus believes that every word spent talking is a prayer left unsaid. Their kill-markings are etched in silver on their pauldrons—precise, orderly, absolute.

**Invader ATV** | 70 pts | Battle-ready (0 XP)
*Piloted by Brother-Initiate Corvus Relentless*

## Crusade Log

*The Crusade of Binding Chains has entered the Malakor Sector. The chains are forged. The vows are spoken. Let the Emperor's enemies tremble.*

**814.M42 — The Defence of Aethelgard**
The Sons of Decay launched a surprise assault on the Knight World of Aethelgard. The Crusade of Binding Chains met them upon the ramparts of Fortress Ironhollow and annihilated the traitor force to the last plague-bearer. The Emperor's Champion fell in single combat against the enemy Lord of Poxes—a sacrifice that bought victory. The Knight Houses have sworn fealty. Aethelgard's defences are reinforced. In the Chapel of Eternal Vigil, the brothers kneel and pray for the visions that will reveal the next Champion.
